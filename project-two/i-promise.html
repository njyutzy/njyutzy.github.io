"I Promise"

Getting off the ground in 2011, LeBron teamed up with the Akron Public Schools to provide the necessary resources and tools to 1,300 students from within the district.

In addition, LeBron tapped on the shoulder of the University of Akron to help out with the program and they answered in a big way, promising every student who graduates a free tuition scholarship.

LeBron has consistently claimed that this is his crowning achievement thus far in his life, beyond any accomplishment on the court.
