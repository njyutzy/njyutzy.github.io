"Pro Career"

There’s no questioning the greatness of LeBron off the court, but perhaps the biggest reason why he’s been able to have that voice is because of his sustained greatness on the court.

Widely acclaimed as the best player in the world for the last 10+ years, LeBron has dominated the NBA and cemented his place in several all-time statistics.

He ranks third all-time in points scored, eighth all-time in assists and most recently won his fourth NBA championship and Finals MVP with the Los Angeles Lakers.

Many people still believe Michael Jordan is the best player of all time, but with a career year at the age of 35, LeBron may have a few more years to claim that highly sought after GOAT status.
