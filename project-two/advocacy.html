"Advocacy"

After advocating and demanding change for the killers of George Floyd, Breonna Taylor and other black Americans earlier this year, LeBron spearheaded a campaign that went beyond protests.

Teaming up with the NAACP, LeBron and other athletes and artists’ were able to successfully register 10,000 volunteer poll workers in primarily Black voting districts as a part of their “More than a Vote” campaign.

Historically, LeBron has been a leading voice in speaking out to social injustice in America.

His outspokenness has been criticized by many and Fox News pundit even made remarks towards LeBron and other athletes saying, “Shut up and Dribble.”
