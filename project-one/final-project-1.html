OHNO BLAZEFACE Regular

(Ohno Blazeface) was designed in 2017 by James Edmondson. It’s part of the larger Ohno Type Company created by Edmondson in 2015. Blazeface is a fatface type style that was meant to be more “chill” than the others according to Edmondson. This mindset is similar to the (Ohno family) in general: a style of fonts created to challenge the norm and recreate a 70s vibe with a modern twist.
